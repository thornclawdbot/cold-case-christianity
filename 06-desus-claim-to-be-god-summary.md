## Key Takeaways

1. **Jesus didn't say "I am God" explicitly** - But He didn't need to; His Jewish audience understood His claims.

2. **"Before Abraham was, I am" caused lethal outrage** - Using God's name from Exodus 3:14 was clear blasphemy to His hearers.

3. **Jesus did what only God can do** - Forgave sins, accepted worship, claimed authority over Law and Sabbath.

4. **His trial reveals His claims** - He quoted Daniel 7 ("Son of Man" receiving worship), causing the high priest to tear his garments.

5. **He was executed for blasphemy** - If His claims weren't clear, His execution makes no historical sense.

---

## Critical Insight

"Jesus didn't have to say the exact phrase 'I am God' any more than a suspect has to say, 'I committed the crime' for investigators to prove it. The evidence itself says it for Him."
