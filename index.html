1. Does Objective Truth Exist?

Summary + Transcript

Can we know truth? J. Warner Wallace argues that just as criminal investigators use evidence to determine what happened in the past, we can use the same methodology to investigate religious claims.

2. Criminal Trials & Moral Truth

Summary + Transcript

If God doesn't exist, objective moral truth doesn't exist either. But our experience of morality suggests it is objective. This chapter explores the connection between moral truth and God's existence.

3. The "Quick Shot" Objection

Summary + Transcript

Critics say religious beliefs don't require the same standards as criminal trials. But if we're making life-altering decisions, shouldn't we apply reasonable evidence standards?

4. The Big Thing About the Big Bang

Summary + Transcript

Everything that begins to exist has a cause. The universe began to exist. Therefore, the universe has a cause. This chapter explores the cosmological argument for God's existence.

5. Why Forensic Faith Makes a Difference

Summary + Transcript

A "forensic faith" is one based on evidence, not just wishful thinking. This chapter shows why treating Christianity as a cold case transforms it from a leap of faith to a reasonable conclusion.

6. Did Jesus Claim to Be God?

Summary + Transcript

The most important question about Jesus is whether He claimed to be God. This chapter examines the historical evidence for Jesus' claims and the significance of His statements about His identity.
