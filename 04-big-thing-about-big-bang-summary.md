# The Big Thing About the Big Bang

**Original URL:** https://coldcasechristianity.com/writings/the-big-thing-about-the-big-bang/

---

## One-Sentence Summary

Wallace argues Christians should embrace Big Bang Cosmology as evidence FOR God, since the theory's core claim that "everything came from nothing" supports Genesis and points to a Creator.

---

## Key Takeaways

1. **Big Bang ≠ Secularism** - Christians can accept "something from nothing" without accepting naturalism or evolution.

2. **The "big thing" is simple** - Everything had a beginning at a specific point in time. This is the core evidence for God.

3. **Evidence for "something from nothing":**
   - Second Law of Thermodynamics
   - Universe expansion
   - Cosmic Radiation Echo
   - The impossibility of infinite regress

4. **Naturalists try to escape it** - The creation implication bothers secular scientists more than Christians.

5. **Age of universe is separate** - The Big Bang proves a beginning, not a specific age.

---

## Critical Insight

"Big Bang Cosmology proposes that everything in the universe came from nothing at a fixed point in the distant past. This point of origin (a cosmological singularity) is entirely consistent with the Christian view of God's creation as described in Genesis 1."

---

## Practical Application

When Christians say "Big Bang is secular," redirect: "The theory says everything came from nothing—exactly what Genesis says. The interpretation, not the evidence, is the issue."

---

## What Wallace Clarifies

- Accept: Something from nothing at a fixed point
- Reject: Naturalism, Evolution, Specific age claims
- Separate: Cosmology from biological evolution
